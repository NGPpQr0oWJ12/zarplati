# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary user is an accountant working at an office desk with employees arriving one by one to receive a payout. Employees interact only with the signature surface on a tablet or touch laptop.

## Product Purpose

The product creates local payroll payout statements from a simple Excel or CSV file, records who has received money, stores a handwritten signature, and exports an Excel report showing received and pending payouts.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, restrained. The interface should feel like a clean internal accounting tool: obvious, calm, and fast under repetitive use.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, bright marketing colors, heavy cards, decorative charts, noisy sidebars, and excessive logs or background processes. Do not make the employee-facing signature flow feel like a complex admin panel.

## Design Principles

- Optimize for the payout queue: search, open, sign, confirm.
- Keep money and signature actions explicit and hard to confuse.
- Prefer large touch targets and direct labels over compact density on tablets.
- Store only useful operational data: payout rows, signatures, timestamps, and reports generated on demand.
- Make status visible at a glance without showing amounts in live search results.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and tablet-friendly pointer input. Russian-language labels must be concise and unambiguous.
